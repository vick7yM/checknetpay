Estimates, Not Guarantees

The figures produced by our calculator are estimates. Your actual paycheck deductions will be determined by:

  • Your specific W-4 withholding elections
  • Your employer's payroll system and pay period timing
  • Pre-tax benefit elections (health insurance, dental, vision, FSA, commuter benefits, etc.) that we may not have complete information about
  • State and local tax elections and exemptions specific to your situation
  • Multiple income sources, which affect total withholding in ways our calculator cannot model
  • Retroactive payroll adjustments, garnishments, or other employer-specific deductions

The only authoritative statement of your actual withholding is your pay stub from your employer.
