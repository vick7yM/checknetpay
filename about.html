About CheckNetPay

CheckNetPay is a free online paycheck calculator built for everyday American workers, job seekers, freelancers, and anyone who wants to understand exactly what they take home after taxes -- before the money hits their bank account.

Our Mission

Most people are offered a salary, handed a number, and left to figure out on their own how much of it they'll actually see. Federal income tax, state income tax, Social Security, Medicare, and benefit deductions can silently take 25–40% of a paycheck -- often more.

We built CheckNetPay to change that. Our goal is simple: give anyone -- from a new graduate accepting their first job offer to a seasoned professional comparing two positions across different states -- an immediate, accurate picture of their real take-home pay.

No registration required. No ads blocking the calculator. No upsells. Just your number.

Accuracy and Limitations

CheckNetPay uses current 2026 federal tax brackets, FICA rates, standard deductions, and state income tax rates updated with IRS Rev. Proc. 2025-32. Social Security wage base: $184,500. 401(k) limit: $24,500.

Your actual net pay may differ because our calculator does not account for:

  • Itemized deductions beyond the standard deduction
  • Tax credits (Child Tax Credit, Earned Income Credit, etc.)
  • All local city and county taxes -- major jurisdictions are included, smaller municipalities may vary
  • Wage garnishments or mandatory post-tax deductions
  • Multiple jobs or variable income

For payroll decisions affecting real paychecks, always verify with your actual pay stub or consult a qualified tax professional.
