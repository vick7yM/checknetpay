Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

Overview

CheckNetPay ("we", "us", or "our") operates checknetpay.com. This page explains how we handle information when you use our free paycheck calculator.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal financial information. All calculations happen entirely in your browser. We never see:

  • Your salary or income figures
  • Your tax deductions or withholdings
  • Your name, email, or any personal information
  • Your results or calculation history

What We Do Use

Google Analytics (if enabled): We may use Google Analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns — how many visitors we get, which pages are popular, and what devices are used. This data is anonymous and never tied to individuals.

Google AdSense: We display ads through Google AdSense. Google uses cookies to show relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized ads at google.com/settings/ads.

Cookies

Our calculator itself uses no cookies. Google AdSense and Analytics may set cookies for their own purposes as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

CheckNetPay is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
